Wayde Crawford

Founder and Producer

Young Sons Records is a small recording label from Corvallis Oregon ran by Wayde Crawford. Wayde attended Oregon State University, obtaining a Music Production and performance degree. With over a decade of experience recording and performing, Young Sons Records specializes in perfecting guitar tones and hip hop based songs.
