Interested in information about our Contract? Check out some of the main points.
1. In agreeing to this contract the Client agrees that Young Sons Records will own the Masters of recorded songs. Extending to inclusions of any performances recorded in the studio. This contract will also serve as permission for Young Sons Records to use Client names, musician names, and photographs in future advertisements. Young Sons Records will allow Clients to use, distribute, and control their content.
2. The Client agrees that any obligations, contracts, or agreements before signing this agreement will not interfere with carrying out this agreement to its full intent and purpose. It is understood and agreed that Young Sons Records will be allowed to terminate and cancel this agreement without liability to Client with or without cause.
3. The client agrees that there is no copyrighted material in any pre-recorded content that may be used in the final Master. This includes samples, melodies, lyrics, or anything with illegitimate nature. Young Sons Records will not be responsible for receiving permission for use of copyrighted material unless otherwise stated. And will not be held responsible for any repercussions that may occur from the use of copyrighted material.
4. Young Sons Records will not release recording files or copies of Clients or Client’s representative until all monies due to the engineer and producer have been paid. If not paid within 30 days, the Client’s recording files left on premises shall become the property of Young Sons Records. Upon complete payment of all monies due by Client, the engineer will transfer all recordings and copyrights to the client if applicable. All songwriting copyrights will remain with the author of the work.
5. If Clients must cancel sooner than 36 hours before their session they are required to pay one half of their estimated session time for the canceled day. This fee is required to be paid before receiving the masters of any songs.
6. If a recording engineer at Young Sons Records must cancel a session due to illness or other reasonable cause, the engineer will reschedule the session for the earliest available time consistent with the needs of the Client.
7. Recording time included setup time, break downtime, and any breaks taken by the Client(s) or engineer. The engineer will be allowed a break of up to 15 minutes every 2 hours. The engineer will be allowed one 30 minute meal break per session every 4 hours. Engineer’s 15-minute breaks will be on the clock, while the 30-minute meal break will be off the clock. The client may leave premises or take any breaks but must pay Young Sons Records for all hourly work.
8. Young Sons Records shall endeavor to secure all property left on premises by Client, but is not responsible for loss due to theft, fire, or other occurrences beyond its control.
9. The Client shall be responsible for any loss or damage of Young Sons Records property of any kind incurred by Client, employees of Client, guests of Clent, or agents of Clent, as a result of misuse, negligence, and or carelessness. The client will be fined for the price of product or equivalent and be required to pay fine before receiving masters.
10. Client and all guests of Client will be respectful and visibly sober during their session, no horseplay, foul play, snooping, explicit behavior, destruction, or substance abuse of any kind on the premises. Client and all guests of Client will remain in the studio, living area, kitchen, bathroom, or any place outside of Young Sons Records. Young Sons Records reserves the right to reject, remove anyone who violates any laws or rules in this contract for any reason.
There is more to our contract, so if you want more information, please send us an email.
